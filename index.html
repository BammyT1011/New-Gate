GATE initiative is a fast growing youths led non profit organisation, founded in 2016, winner of 2017 best NGO Award in Kwara state,Nigeria. GATE Initiative has positively touched over 4000 lives in different parts of Nigeria with over 100 Registered Ambassador’s.

Read More

Our Vision

To create a world where youth will be true to themselves and proud of both their talent and educational achievement.

Our Mission

To encourage, educate and empower; children, teenagers, and youths in discovering, developing and exhibiting their talents alongside balancing it with their education.

Our Objectives

The foundation aim is to achieve these objectives:
a. To identify youths with natural and exceptional potentials but lack opportunity to explore it.
b.To ensure that these youths are trained and equipped with both their natural sills and education.
c.To empower youths through this special initiative so that they can fit well into the labour market anywhere in the world.
d.To partner with other youths networks to curb youths duress through sustained campaign and training.

Our Strategies

CHILDREN OUTREACH AND ADVOCACY

Since inception of GATE Initiative in 2016, we are dedicated to reaching out to children in rural n urban communities, orphanages ,schools and boastal homes with the aim of showing love with our little quota and teaching morals, values and also making them aware of their talents at an early age..

TEENAGERS SENTITIZATIONS AND OUTREACHES

The teenage stage is one of the best time to form, inform and transform the mind of young people.
GATE Initiative has dedicated time , resources and energy to
encourage,educate and equip this category.
We make this happen by visiting secondary schools,we are invited to speak at event pertaining them and we engaged them through our GATE Initiative teens outreach (GITO)

TALENTS HUNT, CAMPAIGN AND COACHING

GATE initiative is committed to setting stages and platforms that allows young people showcase their talents,we are eager to hunt talents,we campaign for talents discovery, development and exhibition.
Our Ambassador’s are also talent coaches and mentors to thousands of young people

YOUTH EDUCATION, DEVELOPMENT AND EMPOWERMENT

No doubt,the future of every society lies in the hands of their youth, We empower young people by encouraging them to take charge of their lives. We do this by addressing their situation and then take action in order to improve their access to resources and transform their consciousness through their beliefs, values, and attitudes. Our aim is to improve quality of life., Uplifting the underprivileged young generation of the society right from providing them with basic education to create a strong foundation for their careers, to developing personality skills,
This is achieved through organizing project that will improve youth development and empowerment

Who We Are

GATE initiative is a fast growing youths organisation in Nigeria. It was founded in 2016.The organization started in the University of Ilorin by some group of students pioneered by Adebayo George ,a sociology student of the citadel. Utilizing the potential that is offered by the energy, curiosity to learn and creative minds of the young people is essential for moving forward in the right direction. As such, we believe that through empowering and motivating the young individuals from all strata of life, we will be able to make a difference in the years to come.GATE is an acronym for Gifts, Ability, Talents and Education. A non- governmental organisation. Neither religious nor political. Dedicated to work with male and female. children, teenagers and youths who are out and in school but believe in their Talents, Gifts, Abilities and Education. GATE initiative has being addressing social issues like poverty,unemployment,teenage pregnancy and criminal activities using effective strategies.The organisation has also mainstreamed education,leadership, entrepreneurship,talent and leadership developments in her corporate strategy and this drives the organisation to invest time, resources, expertise and resources in numerous project to facilitate socioeconomic development of the world.

Why do we exist?
The future of every society can be pictured, analyzed and visualized in the life of the youth. Talents and education are not waste to be thrown away. We are known to pushing out the GATE in you…….

Problem defination
Nigeria a great nation ,filled with unique human resources, lots of Nigerians feel very proud about the abundance of talents in the country.However, there are few Nigerian that are among the greatest in sports, science, art, or any major field at all ,why is that? With brain drain being an issue. Certain factors are preventing gifted young Nigerians from reaching their potential Most parents already have a picture of What they think their children should do in life, based on what they think is best for them in terms of prestige, societal expectations, status e.t.c and not valueing his /her gifts. The Environment: People in the environment learn from experience that the society does not favour creativity and does not reward talents, so they don’t feel motivated to work and develop it.. A consequence of all these systemic problems is that Nigeria is lagging behind on a global level in nearly every field despite its enormous potential. It’s pathetic to see youth within Nigeria suffering from motivation, identity crisis (who am i),sense of hopelessness e.t.c. The parents and the government need to be supported in lifting the future generation.

This initiative, is born out of the need of selfless service to humanity at the University of Ilorin, and moving round all campuses and states in Nigeria and aiming to equip and empower the youth so that t hey can be pillars of the Nigeria economy.

Needs Assessment
Youths in Nigeria need a platform to discover, develop and exhibit their potentials. Many of this youth seek greener pastures abroad thereby risking their lives because they lack opportunity to explore and demonstrate their creative and intelligence. This initiative will help to connect youths to their long sort opportunity and explore the world of entrepreneurship.

Solution/Strategy
GATE Initiative is born to educate and encourage the youths via her social media handles, organizing effective programs, talents shows, counselling,motivational events,workshops, summits,campaigns etc.Using proven, committed faculties and methodologies. Through this events, youths should be able to sustain their lives and contribute meaningfully to the society at large.
